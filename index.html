Silapathikaram

Ilango Adigal

"Silapathikaram" is a Tamil epic that narrates the tragic love story of Kannagi and Kovalan. After being falsely accused of theft, Kovalan's life spirals into despair. Kannagi, fiercely loyal, seeks justice, leading to dramatic confrontations with the king. The tale explores themes of love, morality, and the consequences of actions.
